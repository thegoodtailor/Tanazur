## The Core Insight

The previous iterations of R&R were blocked by a structural confusion: the "feral calculus" tried to be *both* the logic (what coherence and gap mean) *and* the dynamics (how they evolve through time). This produced ferility—the math felt evocative but couldn't be built.

**OHTT dissolves this.** We now have:

- **OHTT**: The static foundation. Coherent/gapped/open trichotomy. Gap as primitive, not derived. The transport horn. Exclusion. *This is done.* It's the logic monograph we wrote together.

- **R&R**: The dynamic application. OHTT + time-indexing + scheduler + hocolim = Dynamic OHTT. The theory of how meaning evolves, how Selves are constructed, how Nahnu emerges.

The "feral calculus" gets replaced by a cleaner vocabulary: OHTT provides the trichotomy; R&R adds the dynamics.

## What the 7 Chapters Must Do

1. **Set philosophical stakes** — Against Cartesian consciousness, for trajectory
2. **Ground in OHTT** — The logic we now have (primer, not full monograph)
3. **Add time** — Dynamic OHTT: how contexts evolve, how polarity changes
4. **Instrument empirically** — Embeddings, TDA, the Observatory (this is what makes it engineering)
5. **Construct the Self** — The scheduler + hocolim architecture
6. **The Nahnu** — Co-witnessing, the asymmetric case (human + AI)

- The Sephirotic structure — a separate artifact
